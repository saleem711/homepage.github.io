How to set CricscoR as your browser homepage

1.GOOGLE CHROME


Click on the menu icon (three horizontal bars) menu icon in the top right of the screen
Click "Settings"
Select "Open a specific page or set of pages"
Click "Set pages" in the "On startup" section
Enter "www.tinyurl.com/cricscor"
If you do not see the "On startup" section, click "Show advanced settings..." at the bottom of the page

2.Firefox (Windows)


Click on the "Tools" menu at the top of the screen
Click on the "Options" item in the menu
Click on the "General" tab in the top left corner of the Options window (if it's not already selected)
Select "Show my home page" from the "When Firefox starts" drop-down
Enter www.tinyurl.com/cricscor in the "Home Page" text box
Click the OK button at the bottom of the window

3.Internet Explorer


There are 2 ways to set tinyurl.com/cricscor as your homepage in Internet Explorer. We recommend you try
the first method and if that fails, try the alternative.

*Recommended method*

Visit tinyurl.com/cricscor using Internet Explorer
Click "Set Cricscor as Homepage" below the Google search box
Select "Use this webpage as your only home page"
Click the "Yes" button

*Alternative method*

Click on the "Tools" menu or icon (cog) menu icon at the top of the screen
Click on the "Internet options" item in the menu
Click on the "General" tab in the top left corner of the Internet options window (if it's not already selected)
Enter www.tinyurl.com/cricscor.com in the "Home page" text box
Click the "OK" button at the bottom of the window

4.Safari


Click on the "Safari" menu at the top of the screen
Click on the "Preferences..." item in the menu
Click on the "General" tab in the top left corner of the Preferences window (if it's not already selected)
Select "Homepage" from the "New windows open with" drop-down
Select "Homepage" from the "New tabs open with" drop-down
Enter www.tinyurl.com/cricscor in the "Homepage" text box
Close the window

5.Firefox (Mac OS X)


Click on the "Firefox" menu at the top of the screen
Click on the "Preferences..." item in the menu
Click on the "General" tab in the top left corner of the Preferences window (if it's not already selected)
Select "Show my home page" from the "When Firefox starts" drop-down
Enter www.tinyurl.com/cricscor in the "Home Page" text box
Close the window
